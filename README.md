# BangerBoy — Pitch Deck

## 1. Title
- BangerBoy: The Stock Market for Viral Content
- Tagline: Invest in the Hype. Own the Moment.

## 2. Problem
- Viral creators capture short-lived spikes, not long-term value
- Fans can’t invest in culture they spot early
- Social hype is massive, but value capture is broken

## 3. Solution
- Turn viral posts into tradable assets
- Creators earn recurring royalties
- Fans/investors profit from early discovery

## 4. Product Snapshot
- Search any post or keyword
- One-click invest or pioneer a new banger
- Portfolio dashboard with P&L and royalties

## 5. How It Works
- Each post becomes a unique Banger asset
- Bonding curve sets price + ensures liquidity
- Graduates to DEX at market cap threshold

## 6. Why Now
- Creator economy maturing, monetization still fragile
- Trends move faster than traditional markets
- Crypto rails make micro-trading instant and global

## 7. Market Opportunity
- Massive TAM across social + creator economy
- Initial focus: Twitter/X + TikTok
- Wedge: viral posts as a new asset class

## 8. Business Model
- 2% fee per trade
- 1.0% platform, 0.9% creator, 0.1% pioneer
- Points & airdrops drive engagement

## 9. Traction (Pre-Seed)
- MVP alpha building now
- Early community in Telegram
- Creator interest + pipeline forming

## 10. Go-To-Market
- Creator onboarding + viral launch moments
- Social-native distribution (X + TikTok)
- Pioneer incentives to reward discovery

## 11. Competition
- Creator platforms = one-off payments
- Meme tokens = fragmented & duplicated
- BangerBoy = 1 post → 1 asset + royalties

## 12. Roadmap + Ask
- MVP: Twitter-only launch
- Scale: TikTok/IG + DEX graduation
- Ask: Pre-seed $X for product, liquidity, growth
